How does a standing desk improve posture?

Posture refers to how the body is held. Standing is a position where the body is supported by the feet.

Benefits of good posture include:

  • Reduced headaches
  • Better joint health
  • Less muscle pain
  • Improved digestion
  • Increased energy

While a standing desk alone cannot improve posture, it can be beneficial. Keeping the body in constant movement helps improve posture over time.

Sitting for too long adds pressure to the body muscles, resulting mostly in neck and back pain. Many people slouch over their screens, exacerbating the issue.

Some related pains include:

  • Back pain
  • Increased stress on the back, neck, arms, and legs
  • Overstretched spinal ligaments from slouching
  • Strained spinal disks

By alternating sitting and standing, the body adapts to the right position. When standing, some of the pressure that arises in the spine during long periods of sitting is relieved.

Sitting for too long has been shown to increase the risks of:

  • Obesity
  • Diabetes
  • Cardiovascular issues
  • Colon and other cancers

Beyond that, sedentary lifestyles lead to weakened abs and hip flexors, varicose veins, and weakened gluteal muscles.

What constitutes good posture?

The spine has a slight S curve and is designed to be erect when standing. Good posture means:

  • Shoulders back
  • Ears aligned over shoulders
  • Hips aligned with shoulders
  • Feet flat on the ground, shoulder-width apart

Posture involves more than just spinal alignment. Here are a few techniques to incorporate into your daily routine:

  • Knees slightly bent to maintain circulation and reduce pressure on the lower back
  • Shift weight from one foot to the other periodically
  • Use a cushioned floor mat to decrease strain on knees, feet, and legs

Good posture depands mainly on the ways the body is aligned. An unconfortable chair with an static table which does does not fit ones body requirement is going to affect the body posture resulting these condition, otherwise a standing desk with adjustable height is going to fit the user need with an appropriate heght to improve comfort.
